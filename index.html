Research

Research Questions & Goals

Three empirical questions guide evaluation of the system's core capabilities.

RQ1

Classification Accuracy

How accurately can fine-tuned DeBERTa-v3 classify sustainability news into environmental themes compared to zero-shot and keyword baselines?

🎯 Target: F1 ≥ 0.80 on 300-article labeled test set
RQ2

RAG Summary Quality

Can a RAG-based system generate coherent, source-grounded trend summaries for a specified theme, region, and time window?

🎯 Target: G-Eval relevance + coherence ≥ 4.0 / 5.0
RQ3

Hybrid Retrieval Gain

Does hybrid retrieval (dense vector + keyword BM25) outperform single-mode retrieval for sustainability domain queries?

🎯 Target: MRR & precision@5 improvement over dense-only
